Woman with Baskets

by Anita Magsaysay-Ho

The painting Women with Baskets by Anita Magsaysay-Ho is a prominent Filipino artist known for her Social Realist and post-Cubist portrayals of women in Filipino culture. The artwork depicts four women carrying baskets, symbolizing the burdens and responsibilities women often carry in their lives. The muted earth colors and subtle details create a rich visual experience, capturing the essence of the subjects with grace and strength. This piece is significant not only for its artistic merit but also for its personal connection, as it was personally gifted by the artist to a distinguished family, reflecting deep friendship and artistic collaboration.
